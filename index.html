About me

Much of my time over the last few years has been spent learning essential skills on my own accord. Through books, online courses, online tutorials, and trial-and-error during work on various self-assigned projects.

During those 8 months, I spent time working on an engineering team performing requirements analysis, design, and implementation of a large-scale web service for their next generation of vehicle technology.

Day-to-day activities involve coding, working on CI\CD pipelines, and interacting with various pieces of technology including Spinnaker, Jenkins, Kubernetes, and Helm.
